My Travel Blog

Last month, I visited the beautiful sea beach of Cox’s Bazar with my friends. The journey was full of fun, laughter, and memorable experiences. From the refreshing ocean breeze to the sound of crashing waves, everything felt magical. The golden sand stretched endlessly, inviting us for long walks by the shore. We soaked in the vibrant local culture, meeting friendly fishermen and watching colorful boats sail by. I’ll share a bit of my adventure with you.

We started our journey early in the morning by bus and reached by noon. After checking into our hotel, we headed straight to the beach, where the sun was shining brightly and the waves gently kissed the shore. The sunset was absolutely breathtaking orange, pink, and purple hues painting the sky. Later, we enjoyed delicious street food like fried hilsa and spicy chotpoti at a bustling market nearby. We also went shopping for souvenirs, picking up handmade bracelets and shell necklaces. we listened to music under the starlit sky, sharing stories and laughter until it was time to rest. The peaceful sound of waves lulled us to sleep, promising another exciting day ahead.
